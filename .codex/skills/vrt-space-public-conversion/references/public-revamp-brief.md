## Public Goal

Make VRT SPACE feel like a focused SaaS product that helps users:

- understand what is wrong with their website
- see what to fix next
- improve search and AI visibility over time

## Messaging Rules

- Speak in outcomes, not in platform internals.
- Use plain language.
- Keep the service feeling premium without inflated claims.
- Make the visitor feel:
  - this understands my website
  - this shows me what is missing
  - this gives me a path forward

## Required Homepage Flow

1. Hero with clear promise and CTA
2. Short value explanation
3. Three blocks for Audit, SEO, and AEO
4. Simple process explanation
5. Workspace or progress concept
6. Trust section
7. Final CTA

## CTA Rules

- Keep one dominant action language across the page.
- Reduce equal-weight buttons.
- Use the secondary CTA only to support the primary one.
- Keep forms short and progressive.

## Visual Rules

- Calm and premium, not loud
- Strong hierarchy
- Clean spacing
- Minimal distractions
- Mobile-friendly first

## Things To Hide Or Downplay

- Internal platform architecture
- Extra modules as top-level public options
- Dashboard-style clutter
- over-technical copy
- too many product paths in the header
