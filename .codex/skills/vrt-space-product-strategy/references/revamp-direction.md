## Product Story To Preserve

The product should feel like one focused SaaS loop:

Audit -> SEO -> AEO -> Workspace -> Rerun -> Progress -> Retention

Audit is the first wow moment.
SEO explains what is missing.
AEO frames the modern AI-visibility edge.
Workspace makes the value persistent.
Reruns make improvement visible.

## Public Scope Rules

- Keep the public-facing product focused on Audit, SEO, and AEO.
- Hide or de-emphasize extra modules from first-touch marketing pages.
- Make the site feel like a focused product, not a generic agency website.
- Keep copy outcome-led, plain, and conversion-oriented.
- Use one clear CTA family across the public journey.

## Workspace Rules

- Treat each workspace as a growth log for one site or business context.
- Keep project history, reruns, credits, and before/after movement visible.
- Make progress over time more important than feature count.
- Make credits feel like access to progress, not punishment.

## Strategy Questions

Use these checks before approving scope:

1. Does this help users understand value faster?
2. Does this improve conversion, retention, or scalability?
3. Does this keep the experience simple?
4. Does this fit the Audit -> SEO -> AEO -> Workspace loop?

If not, simplify or defer it.

## Public Experience Targets

- Fast understanding in the first 3-5 seconds
- One primary CTA on each screen
- Calm, premium, trustworthy feel
- Clear distinction between public marketing and authenticated product work
- Better sequencing rather than more modules
