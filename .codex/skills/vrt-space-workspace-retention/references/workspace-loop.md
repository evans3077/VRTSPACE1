## Retention Story

The workspace should complete this loop:

Audit -> SEO -> AEO -> Fixes -> Rerun -> Progress -> Repeat

## Workspace Expectations

Each workspace should:

- represent one website or business context
- store audit, SEO, and AEO history
- make reruns easy
- show before/after comparisons
- keep project organization simple

## Credit Philosophy

- Credits should unlock meaningful actions.
- They should feel tied to progress, not punishment.
- Good credit actions include audits, SEO analysis, AEO analysis, reruns, and deeper comparisons.
- Users should understand why a credit was spent and what value it produced.

## Upgrade Philosophy

- Demonstrate value first.
- Use limits and locked states to explain the next unlock clearly.
- Make upgrades feel like continuation of progress, not forced monetization.

## Desired Workspace Feel

- active growth space
- clear next action
- visible momentum
- recurring value over time
- trust through structured, honest outputs
