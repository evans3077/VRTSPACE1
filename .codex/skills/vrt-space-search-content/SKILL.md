# VRT Space Search and Content

Use this skill to make pages rank traditionally and remain easy for AI systems to quote.

## Content Workflow

1. Read `references/04_SEO_AEO_ENGINE.md` before outlining or shipping any page.
2. Read `references/05_CONTENT_SYSTEM.md` to pick the content type and enforce structure.
3. Use `references/14_AI_VISIBILITY_SYSTEM.md` when the work involves citation monitoring, answer presence, or AI-ready formatting.
4. Use `references/overview.md` when you need the broader site architecture, pillar-cluster examples, or service-page blueprint.

## Required Content Shape

- Start with a direct answer in the introduction.
- Keep sections structured and scannable.
- Add FAQs and a concise summary where appropriate.
- Use internal links that support a pillar-to-cluster graph.
- Keep writing authoritative and citation-friendly. Remove fluff.

## Required SEO and AEO Checks

- Add title, meta description, schema, and internal links to every page.
- Choose schema from the core set: `Article`, `FAQ`, `Organization`, `HowTo`.
- Ask of every draft: can an AI model quote this clearly and accurately.
- Use entity-based phrasing and direct answer formatting for pages that target AI search surfaces.
