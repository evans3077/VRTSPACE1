Technical & Strategic Gaps

What is the difference between SEO and AEO in the VRTSPACE ecosystem?
SEO focuses on organic traffic through traditional rankings, while AEO focuses on securing authoritative citations in AI responses.

Traditional SEO optimizes for the way search engines index and rank pages for users to click. AEO optimizes for the way AI models (LLMs) ingest, summarize, and cite information. VRTSPACE unifies these disciplines so that your website remains dominant in traditional search results while becoming a primary source for generated AI answers.

What is 'Semantic Authority' and why is it the goal of the Platform?
Semantic Authority is the measure of how completely your domain covers the entities and factual relationships of a topic.

Search engines and AI models no longer just look for matching strings; they look for matching *things*. By establishing Semantic Authority, you tell search engines that your site is the definitive "source of truth" for a topic, making you the primary candidate for citation across all search surfaces.

How does the Authority Engine track 'Citation Velocity'?
Citation Velocity measures how rapidly your brand is gaining new mentions in AI search responses.

Visibility is a moving target. We track how often LLMs choose your domain as the "ground truth" over time. A rising Citation Velocity indicates that your AEO optimizations are working and that your site is being prioritized in the latest model training and retrieval windows.

Can I use the workspace modules independently?
Yes, our platform is modular by architectural design to allow for targeted growth injections.

Every business is at a different stage of authority. You can lock in the SEO, AEO, or Content Forge modules as needed, allowing you to focus on the specific metrics that will drive your next stage of growth without being forced into a single monolithic path.

How do custom build paths work for high-performance apps?
We treat every custom app build as a high-performance search surface.

Traditional app architecture often ignores SEO and AEO until after deployment. Our build path integrates our Authority Engine from day one, ensuring that your app's foundation is fast, crawlable, and natively supports the technical schema required for AI discovery.
