Frequently asked

Common questions about VRTSPACE.

What is AI Search Visibility (AEO) and why does it matter?
AEO ensures your brand is the definitive answer cited by AI agents like Perplexity, ChatGPT, and Google Gemini.

As the search landscape shifts from "ten blue links" to generated direct answers, traditional SEO is no longer enough. Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) focuses on structuring your data and authority so that LLMs can ingest, synthesize, and cite your content. Without AEO, your brand risks becoming invisible in the "zero-click" search environments of tomorrow.

How precisely does VRTSPACE calculate the 'Authority Gap'?
The Authority Gap is a proprietary metric measuring the semantic completeness of your content vs. top market leaders.

Our engine crawls the top 50 citations in your industry and builds a "Ground Truth" entity map. We then audit your domain against this map to see exactly which facts, schema, or structural elements you are missing to win the citation box. This identifies the exact informational voids that are currently preventing your brand from being the primary answer.

Does VRTSPACE integrate with my existing tech stack (CMS/CRM)?
Yes. Our workspace outputs are optimized for WordPress, Webflow, Shopify, and Headless CMS architectures.

We provide bridge-ready JSON, HTML units, and structured Markdown for every piece of content generated. This ensures that the high-precision schema.org tags and entity-rich formatting produced in our forge survive the transfer to your production environment without loss of technical integrity.

Is VRTSPACE an agency or a SaaS platform?
We are a hybrid Intelligence Hub designed to bridge the gap between automated tools and custom execution.

VRTSPACE provides high-precision intelligence workspaces (SEO, AEO, Content) for internal growth, while maintaining a dedicated Agency Build Path for projects requiring custom architecture, deep technical rebuilds, or high-complexity enterprise implementation that goes beyond our standard modules.

Is my domain data used to train public AI models?
No. VRTSPACE maintains strict data isolation and enterprise-grade privacy protocols.

All audit data, competitive intelligence, and generated drafts are stored in your encrypted project silo. We do not feed your proprietary content or strategic "Authority Maps" back into public training sets. Your competitive advantage remains exclusively yours.

How does the platform handle global vs. local search intelligence?
We use localized proxy systems to simulate search environments in any target market or language.

Global authority is different from local relevance. Our audits allow you to specify exact geolocation parameters, enabling you to see how your brand is perceived in London vs. New York vs. Tokyo, ensuring your AEO strategy remains accurate across diverse geographic retrieval zones.
