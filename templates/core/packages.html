What to know before you choose a plan

What changes as I move up the plans?
You mainly unlock more audit capacity, stronger SEO and AEO depth, and better reporting continuity.

Higher plans are for teams that need more analysis volume, more saved history, and more dependable reporting support across ongoing work.

Should I start with a paid plan immediately?
Usually the smartest first move is still the audit.

The audit gives you a stronger basis for deciding whether the next priority is technical cleanup, deeper SEO execution, or stronger AEO work.

When does custom scope make sense?
Custom scope makes sense when the visibility problem is broader than a normal operating plan.

Use it for multi-market complexity, unusual reporting requirements, or implementation work that needs a more tailored engagement.
