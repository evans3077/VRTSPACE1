Investment Intelligence

Scaling & ROI Clarity

What is an "Intelligence Credit" and how is it consumed?
Intelligence Credits are the internal currency for high-precision audits, AEO benchmarks, and AI content generation.

Every high-compute operation—such as crawling a competitor's AI citation data or generating rank-ready content—consumes a specific amount of credits. This ensures that you only pay for the high-intensity processing your project requires, while basic dashboard monitoring remain accessible.

Do Intelligence Credits roll over to the next month?
Unused credits from high-tier plans roll over for up to 90 days.

We recognize that data needs fluctuate. If you perform a massive audit one month and focus on human-led execution the next, your rollover credits allow you to maintain access to high-precision snapshots and forge production without friction or loss of investment.

What is the estimated ROI of the Momentum vs. Growth plans?
Momentum is for maintaining authority, while Growth is built to aggressively capture new AI citation boxes.

Most clients on the Growth plan see a 40% faster "Time-to-Citation" because of the higher execution priority and deep competitor profiling limits. Momentum is ideal for established brands protecting territory, while Growth is the standard for contenders aiming for industry dominance.

How do the "Self-Serve" tiers differ from "Enterprise Partner" paths?
Self-Serve tiers empower your internal team, while Enterprise paths are custom-built for large-scale delivery.

Our modular plans (Growth, Scale, Momentum) are perfect for teams using our AI and SEO intelligence to run their own audits. The Enterprise Partner route is specifically for businesses requiring custom architecture or direct scoping from the VRTSPACE core team for success.

Are there bulk discounts for multi-domain enterprise accounts?
Yes, we offer a "Universal Credit Pool" for accounts managing multiple high-authority domains.

Organizations managing 5+ domains are eligible for custom pool-based credit pricing and consolidated billing. This allows you to allocate resources across projects dynamically, reducing the per-domain cost of intelligence and simplifying resource management for global agencies.
