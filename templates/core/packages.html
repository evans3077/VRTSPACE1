When to go custom

Only when the standard product flow is no longer enough.

Custom work is best for larger rebuilds, advanced implementation, or multi-team requirements that go beyond the normal plan ladder.

Best first step

Run the audit before choosing.

The audit gives you a real baseline, which makes pricing and next-step decisions much easier.

Pricing questions

Simple answers before you commit.

Should I start with a plan or with the free audit?
Start with the free audit first.

It gives you a clearer picture of what is broken, what matters most, and whether you need a paid plan right away.

What changes as I move up the plan ladder?
You unlock more capacity, more follow-through, and more room to keep improving.

The higher tiers are designed for teams that need more audits, more active workspace usage, or more advanced delivery support.

When should I request custom scope?
Only when the normal audit, SEO, AEO, and workspace flow is not enough.

If your need is a large rebuild, bespoke implementation, or multi-domain complexity, custom scope becomes the better path.

Can I create a workspace before I upgrade?
Yes. You can start the relationship before committing to a larger plan.

That keeps the first step light and lets the product prove value before you decide how far to scale.
