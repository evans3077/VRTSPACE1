Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers

Is it really free?

Yes. The full audit — score, per-engine breakdown, top 5 fixes — is free forever. No card required.

We make money when teams who like the audit upgrade to track citations over time. If you just want the audit and nothing else, that's fine too.

How long does it take?

Usually 30–60 seconds depending on how many pages your site has and how fast they respond.

The result lands in your inbox and is also viewable in the workspace as soon as it finishes.

What if I'm not sure my site is ready to be audited?

Run it anyway. The audit is designed to surface fixes — if your site is in rough shape, the report will tell you exactly what to fix first. Teams with scores in the 30s and 40s are common starting points.

Do I need to create an account?

No. You can see your report without signing up. If you want to track changes over time, re-run on a schedule, or share dashboards with teammates, that's when an account is useful — but it's optional.

Do you sell or share my data?

Never. We use your URL to run the audit, and your email to send you the report. That's it. We don't sell to data brokers, lead resellers, or ad networks. Read our full privacy policy.

I'm an agency — can I run it on every client?

Yes. Agencies routinely use the free audit as the first slide of every pitch deck — it shows the client where they're invisible in AI search. See our agency page for the workflow.

What's the difference between this and the paid plans?

The free audit gives you a point-in-time snapshot. Paid plans give you:

• Continuous tracking — re-runs on a schedule so you see whether your fixes are working
• Prompt Tracker — monitor specific queries across all 3 engines
• Share-of-voice — benchmark against competitors
• Weekly email digest, team access, white-label reports
