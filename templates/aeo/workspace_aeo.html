AEO Intelligence FAQ

Decoding AI Visibility

How is my AEO Readiness Score calculated?
The AEO Readiness Score is a composite metric focusing on semantic visibility, entity density, technical structure, and informational completeness.

We analyze your content's "answer-ready" density—the ability for AI models to rapidly ingest and cite your information. By benchmarking your technical schema and topical authority against the entities currently winning the "citation box," we provide a high-precision probability of your brand being the primary answer for a specific user query.

How do LLMs (Large Language Models) actually 'discover' my site?
LLMs discover your site through a combination of traditional crawling, massive pre-training datasets, and real-time retrieval blocks.

When an AI agent like Perplexity or SearchGPT answers a query, it often performs a real-time web search (RAG). If your site is correctly structured with high entity-density and technical schema, the LLM's retriever identifies your content as a high-relevance source and uses it to synthesize the final answer.

What are 'Entity Clusters' and how do they impact AEO?
Entity Clusters are groups of related people, places, and things that define a topic's semantic neighborhood.

AI models don't just see words; they see a "Knowledge Graph." If your content lacks the expected neighboring entities for your topic, the model views your expertise as incomplete. VRTSPACE helps you identify these missing entities so you can complete the cluster and win the citation over less comprehensive competitors.

What is the impact of Schema.org on AI retrieval architecture?
Schema.org metadata acts as the primary "Handshake" between your server and an AI retriever agent.

By providing precise JSON-LD (Schema) for your products, FAQs, and articles, you provide a clear, machine-readable summary that the model can ingest with 100% accuracy. This removes the "hallucination risk" for the AI and significantly increases your chances of being quoted as the factual source.

What does 'Citation Coverage' represent in the benchmarks?
Citation Coverage measures the frequency of your brand appearing as a primary source across major LLM search engines.

Unlike traditional search impressions, AEO visibility hinges on being a cited source. Our engine simulates queries across models like Perplexity, SearchGPT, and Gemini to determine if your domain is providing the ground-truth data. High coverage means your content is effectively structured for retrieval and is seen as highly authoritative by AI agents.

How does 'Zero-Click' search impact my conversion attribution?
In the AEO era, conversion shifts from "Link Clicks" to "Brand Citations" and "User Attribution" in AI answers.

If a user gets their answer directly from an AI without visiting your site, traditional tracking fails. We help you track "Indirect Conversion" by monitoring the frequency of your brand being mentioned as the authoritative source, ensuring you get credit for the systemic influence your brand is creating in the discovery layer.
