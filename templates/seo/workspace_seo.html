SEO Intelligence FAQ

Technical & Performance Gaps

What specific technical signals does the SEO audit scan for?
The audit specifically targets 'Authority Killers'—the structural and semantic gaps that hinder indexing and AI discovery.

While generic tools focus on surface-level metadata, the VRTSPACE Technical Audit deep-dives into your site’s DOM hierarchy and indexation logic. We scan for semantic heading discipline, schema.org validation, and mobile performance bottlenecks. By ensuring your site is architecturally sound, we lay the foundation for both traditional organic ranking and high-precision AEO visibility.

What is a 'Phantom Keyword' and how do we find them?
Phantom Keywords are queries where your site is already cited by AI models despite not ranking on Page 1 of Google.

These are your highest-ROI growth opportunities. Our engine cross-references your current SEO rankings with live AEO citation data to find topics where you already have "Semantic Authority." By making small technical SEO adjustments to these specific pages, you can rapidly bridge the gap to Page 1 dominance for queries where you are already the expert.

How precisely does VRTSPACE calculate 'Crawl Budget' efficiency?
We monitor the ratio of "Value-Density Pages" to "Technical Waste" like 404s and redirect chains.

Every domain has a finite capacity for how many pages a search engine will crawl daily. By identifying and pruning low-relevance URLs and fixing redirect loops, we ensure that Googlebot focuses its energy on your high-conversion "Money Pages," significantly speeding up the indexation of new clusters and technical site updates.

How often should I run "Intelligence Updates" for the audit?
Regular updates are critical for maintaining a live map of the 'Authority Gap' between you and your competitors.

The search landscape is highly dynamic. Competitor pushes and algorithm updates can shift the required authority threshold overnight. Running an update after any major architectural deployment or seasonal content push ensures that your SEO strategy remains proactive. This allows you to identify and close gaps before they impact your primary traffic channels.

What is the difference between 'Domain Authority' and 'Semantic Depth'?
Domain Authority measures backlink equity, while Semantic Depth measures topical completeness.

In the current search landscape, linkage alone is no longer enough. You can have high DA but low Semantic Depth, which leads to ranking volatility during algorithm updates. VRTSPACE focuses on the synergy between the two—ensuring your backend technical authority (links) is matched by deep, entity-dense content that algorithms and AI models trust implicitly.

How do we map technical fixes to specific revenue outcomes?
We correlate Core Web Vitals and technical health improvements with historical conversion data.

Technical SEO is often seen as a cost center. VRTSPACE changes this by showing the relationship between page-speed deltas, indexation coverage, and top-of-funnel traffic growth. This allows you to prioritize engineer resources based on the projected ROI of the specific technical lift, rather than just solving errors for the sake of cleanliness.
